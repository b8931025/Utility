SqlParameter like寫法

方法一
cmd.commandText="Select * From Emp Where EmpName like @EmpName ;"; 
cmd.parameters.add("@EmpName",sqldvtype.nvarchar,20).value= "%" + txtEmpName.Text.Trim() + "%"; //過濾前後空白
