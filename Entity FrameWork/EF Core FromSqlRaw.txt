FromSqlRaw

FromSqlRaw只能用在DbSet<>上
var posts = context.Posts.FromSqlRaw("SELECT * FROM dbo.Posts").ToList();

執行預存程序(Stored Procedure)
var posts = context.Posts.FromSqlRaw("EXECUTE dbo.GetNewsPosts").ToList();

使用參數
var user = "John";
var posts = context.Posts.FromSqlRaw("EXECUTE dbo.GetNewsPosts {0}", user).ToList();    //會有SQL injection
var posts = context.posts.FromSqlInterpolated($"EXECUTE dbo.GetNewsPosts {user}").ToList();  //會自動轉換成DbParameter
