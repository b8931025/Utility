問題:
SqlServer安裝或連線失敗，在error log中看到下面錯誤
There have been 256 misaligned log IOs which required falling back to synchronous IO.

問題原因:
Windows 11安裝在SSD上，磁區大小超過4096造成

在命令列執行
fsutil fsinfo c:

如果PhysicalBytesPerSectorForAtomicity和PhysicalBytesPerSectorForPerformance有一個大於4096，就會造成SqlServer無法連線或無法安裝


解決:
New-ItemProperty -Path "HKLM:\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Services\stornvme\Parameters\Device" -Name "ForcedPhysicalSectorSizeInBytes" -PropertyType MultiString -Force -Value "* 4095"

Get-ItemProperty -Path "HKLM:\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Services\stornvme\Parameters\Device" -Name   "ForcedPhysicalSectorSizeInBytes"

重新開機後即可解決，如果還是不行，就把之前新增的schema刪掉
Sqllocaldb delete mssqllocaldb
Sqllocaldb create mssqllocaldb -s
